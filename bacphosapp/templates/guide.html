Site Map

Homepage

The Homepage serves as the entry point to the application, offering users an intuitive search function for quick access. Users select their search criteria and input the corresponding search term.
Search Page

The Search Page hosts the protein list containing all phosphoproteins and their respective phosphosites, along with a search engine. Users can search for phosphosites by UniProt code, gene, or protein name. Detailed information and features such as PDF downloads are accessible via the view button.
Contribute

The Contribute page allows users to submit new phosphoprotein/phosphosite data from their research to our database. Access to the submission form requires registration, and all submissions undergo thorough review before approval, including user registrations. Permission-based access is enforced.
Resources

This page offers a variety of valuable resources related to protein phosphorylation.
Contact

Users can provide feedback or make inquiries through this interface.
About/General Information/Citation

For users utilizing data from pEDB, we kindly request citation of our article for acknowledgment.
