Site Map

Homepage

The Homepage serves as the gateway to the application, offering users a convenient search function to swiftly query Phospho-sites by protein name.
Search Page

The Search Page houses the main and advanced search functionalities, empowering users to search for phospho-sites by UniProt name, gene, or protein name. Additionally, users can retrieve supplementary information related to the protein and access features such as downloading PDFs.
Contribute

The Contribute page enables users to submit new phosphosite data from their own studies or published research to our database. Access to the submission form requires registration. All submissions undergo thorough review before approval.
Resources

This page offers an array of valuable resources pertaining to protein phosphorylation.
Contact

Users can provide feedback or reach out to us with inquiries via this interface.
