PEDB Database

Search E. coli phosphoprotein

PEDB (Phosphorylation Escherichia DataBase) stands as an exhaustive repository for protein phosphorylation data within E. coli, comprising a significant collection of over 2000 phosphoproteins and identifying more than 7000 phospho-sites. This comprehensive dataset represents approximately 40% of the total E. coli proteome, highlighting the crucial role of protein phosphorylation. With its rich array of annotated phospho-sites, this online database serves as an indispensable resource for researchers investigating protein phosphorylation. Moreover, access to the platform is unrestricted and freely available to all users.
