PEDB Database

Search E. coli phosphoprotein

pEDB stands as an exhaustive repository for protein phosphorylation data within E. coli, comprising a significant collection of more than 2000 phosphoproteins and identifying 6256 phospho-sites. This comprehensive dataset represents an estimated 37% of the total E. coli K12 proteome, underscoring its crucial role across diverse cellular processes. With its rich array of annotated phospho-sites, this online database serves as an indispensable resource for researchers investigating protein phosphorylation. Moreover, access to the platform is unrestricted and freely available to all users.
